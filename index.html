Types of Plants

  • Herbs (Examples of Herbs: Maize, rice, mint, coriander, grass, etc)

    • Plants that have soft, green, and perishable stems are called herbs.

  • Shrubs (Examples of Shrubs: Tulsi, Lemon, jasmine, Nerium, china rose, pomegranate, etc)

    • Plants with woody stems, with branches of almost equal size arising from the stem immediately above the soil, are called shrubs.

  • Trees (Examples of Trees: Mango, neem, palm, banyan, etc)

    • Trees are tall and big plants and have one hard, woody stem called a trunk

  • Creepers (Examples of Creepers: Bottle gourd, watermelon, pumpkin, strawberry, etc)

    • Creepers are plants with weak stems and grow horizontally along with the soil or the ground since they cannot stand upright.
